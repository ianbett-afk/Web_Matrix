KABARAK UNIVERSITY

About

BIO DATA

History

Kabarak university was established on an undisclosed year by late former president Daniel Arap Moi that is widely known for its strong academic and religious beliefs that have brought up bright men and women in the kenyan society in various fields of education.

Location

Kabarak University is located approximately 20km away from nakuru town on a 600 acre plot of land where a holistic education with quality is offered.

ACADEMIC PROGRAMS

Schools

Academic programs in kabarak university are spread across the following seven schools:

  1. Education, Humanities and Social Sciences.
  2. Science, Engineering and Technology.
  3. Law.
  4. Business and Ecomonics.
  5. Pharmacy.
  6. Music and Media.
  7. Medicine and health sciences.
